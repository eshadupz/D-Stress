About Us

Welcome! Do you feel stressed or just need a place to relax? Well, you've come to the right place. D-stress is a website that allows users to vent, meditate, and discover tools and techniques to help guide their way through life. Our main goal is to inspire users to have fun, enjoy life, and of course, de-stress.
